Color Festival

Holi is a festival celebrated by Hindus around the world. Holi marks the beginning of spring, and is usually celebrated at the end of February or early March.
Participants in a Holi festival often throw brightly colored powder and scented water at each other.The Colour Festival is a way of celebrating the coming of spring.


Paint Night

We started with one Paint Nite in the basement of a Boston bar in 2012 and it all took off from there. Paint Nite flourished across local communities and soon expanded to include many more creative experiences.

Our goal has remained the same since the beginning: to bring people together over shared, hands-on experiences, whether in-person or virtually.
